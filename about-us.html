About Us

A caring, warm world with better health, wellbeing for all citizens to help and share.

Volunteer Vacations’ is an organization that connects volunteers with the different charities in need, usually at a distance from the volunteer’s home base.
Our dedicated staff is experienced in listening and understanding the volunteers whom contact us, and after meeting with you they will help discuss what charity might be best for you.
Volunteerism can positively impact not only your community, but as well as you – the individual. Most volunteering is taken place in a group setting and many volunteers are specifically trained in the areas they work, such as education, emergency rescue, and others.
Become empowered to impact our world – for the better. The basis for a brighter tomorrow starts today – built on a hand up, not a handout.
