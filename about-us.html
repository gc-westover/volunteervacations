About Us

A caring, warm environment with better health, wellbeing for all community members to help, share, and grow.

Volunteer Vacations is an organization that connects volunteers with the different charities in need, usually at a distance from the volunteer’s home base. Our dedicated staff is experienced in listening and understanding the volunteers whom contact us, and after meeting with you they will help discuss what charity might be best for you.

Volunteerism can positively impact not only your community, but as well as you – the individual. Most volunteering is taken place in a group setting and many volunteers are specifically trained in the areas they work, such as education, emergency rescue, and others.

Become empowered to impact our world – for the better. The basis for a brighter tomorrow starts today – built on a hand up, not a handout.

What we're about

Our 'Partners' page contains all of the information regarding the organizations we represent, work alongside, and give you the chance to make a positive impact.

Ready to take action? Our 'Take Action' page houses the volunteer opportunities we have available. You can also Donate or Become a Host for our volunteers who are vacationing and volunteering away from their home.

Still unsure where to start? The 'Contact Us' page is there for you to reach out with any comments or questions.

Happy Volunteering!

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: What states do you offer volunteer opportunities in?
Answer: All of New England and New York state.

Question: I would like to help, but don't want to leave town. How may I help?
Answer: You may interested in hosting volunteers. Navigate to our 'Take Action' page to find out more.

Question: What are examples of some of your volunteer opportunities?
Answer: We have teamed up with an array of organizations such as the Red Cross and assist with Blood Drives and Habitat for Humanities to assist building housing in needed areas. We also are looking for volunteers for soup kitchens, delivering food on a daily basis, helping out at an animal shelter, tutoring a young child in need, becoming a crisis counselor, and plenty more.

Question: What will I gain from volunteer work?
Answer: By volunteering you will helping out a community in need. You can work alongside skilled laborers who volunteer their time and expertise, thus you can learn tips and tricks regarding the volunteer vacation you select.

Question: Do you offer college credits in exchange for volunteering?
Answer: Unfortunately, we do not at this time.
